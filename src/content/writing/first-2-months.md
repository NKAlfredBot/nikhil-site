I've been on sabbatical for ~ 10 weeks. What have I been up to?

I took a 3 week road trip with my dad through national parks and open country.

![Road trip map](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2fMq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5dd00873-ebf1-4c3e-b3f4-33043de4243a_1600x690.png)

I got a personal trainer and began taking my physical health more seriously.

I helped eviscerate chickens on [a farm](https://kinderhookfarm.square.site/) in upstate NY.

My friend who helps non-profits with marketing and community building wanted to explore how AI might help him, so I [wrote a script](https://github.com/nkumar23/reddit_ai) — with plenty of help from ChatGPT — that passes social media content to GPT-4 following some parameters he had for an upcoming project.

I'm cooking a ton and getting a deep appreciation for seasonal produce in New York thanks to participating in the [Stanton Street CSA](https://stantonstreetcsa.wordpress.com/) for the first time.

I dusted off my [Teenage Engineering](https://teenage.engineering/) gear and started jamming [again](https://soundcloud.com/nkumar23/quarantinedreams?utm_source=clipboard&utm_medium=text&utm_campaign=social_sharing).

I could keep going – not because I've been hyper productive but because my only explicit goals during this time have been to 1) exercise and 2) make something every day. Consistency has been the focus; rebuilding habits > seeking any particular outcome. As a result, I've often found myself chasing down answers to questions burrowing in my mind each day; the only requirement is learning by doing versus learning passively.

I'll use this space in the next few weeks to document some of the ideas and projects I've been noodling on and will elaborate on some of the stuff above :)
