[Coursera to Chef](https://medium.com/@nkumar23/i-m-leaving-coursera-to-become-a-chef-4e4de0fee1e9) - This piece is a reflection on my decision to go from the tech world into the kitchen, and the meta-skills that I was developing along the way.

[The Untold Story of Indians in America](https://medium.com/@nkumar23/the-untold-story-of-indians-in-america-e000cccb6e95) - Did you know Indians were banned from migrating to the USA for a good chunk of the 20th century? I didn't until I did some research on Indian American immigration, and wrote about what I learned in this short essay originally sent to The Listserve.

[Learning to Swim](http://onwardsandbeyond-blog.tumblr.com/post/32801878048/learning-to-swim) - I learned how to swim when I was 24 while at a friend's family's house during a backpacking trip in Africa and Europe. I wrote about that experience and the process of learning a new skill.

I wrote about [my mom](http://onwardsandbeyond-blog.tumblr.com/post/34193522585/convergence-happy-birthday-amma) and [my dad](http://onwardsandbeyond-blog.tumblr.com/post/53125288483/memories-of-my-fathers-mumbai) while moving to Mumbai, where they were both born. They've played a large role in shaping who I am today, but our relationship has also evolved as I've gotten older and relate to them in new ways. I write about both ideas in these two essays.
