[Microbial Memories](https://www.timeplaceculture.com) is a reflection on the possibilities of microbiomes to be used as a form of cultural archive. Based on the emerging science linking microbiomes to terroir in wine and other foods, we imagine a future where we can capture place and time with the microorganisms living in that moment.

We initially presented this project as a submission in the 2020 Biodesign Competition, where we won the Science Sandbox Prize for Public Engagement.

We then developed a [workshop](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/microbial-memories-a-speculative-design-workshop-tickets-137323507307) with [Genspace](https://www.genspace.org/) to further develop our research into speculative design and archival practices of man and nature.
